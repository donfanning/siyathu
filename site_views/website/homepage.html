Siyathu

Siyathu is a live video mixing app. Siyathu reduces the cost of high end hardware and software by providing a lightweight infrastructure to live video production. Siyathu is capable of live mixing and switching between video sources including cameras, mobile phones, and video clips. You can simply start a live video stream by just starting a channel and connecting some video sources. Siyathu brings live video production simple and easy.
